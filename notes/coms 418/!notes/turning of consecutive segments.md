#coms418 
- Segments $\overline{p_0p_1},\overline{p_1p_2}$ move form $p_0,p_1,p_2$
		- **Counterclockwise**
			- $\overrightarrow{p_0p_1} \times \overrightarrow{p_1p_2} > 0$
		- **Clockwise**
			- $\overrightarrow{p_0p_1} \times \overrightarrow{p_1p_2} < 0$
		- **Turn of 0 or pi**
			- $\overrightarrow{p_0p_1} \times \overrightarrow{p_1p_2} = 0$
